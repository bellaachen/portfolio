About

關於我Who I Am

我是一位偏向 Systems PM 的產品經理：擅長把整個系統攤開來看，找到流程的斷點，建立規則，再把規則寫成工程師可以直接執行的規格。 I'm a systems-oriented Product Manager: I map whole systems, find where processes break, build the rules, and translate them into specs engineers can execute directly.

十年 PM 路徑橫跨金融數據、社群生態系與移動出行，最後在交易平台落地。清算團隊出身的背景，讓我對交易平台的底層 — FIX 協議、後台權限、佣金與返佣邏輯、公司行動 — 有第一手的領域深度。這是多數 feature PM 沒有的視角。 A ten-year PM path across fintech data, community ecosystems, and mobility eventually landed in trading platforms. A clearing-team background gives me first-hand depth in their underside — FIX protocol, back-office permissions, commission and rebate logic, corporate actions. It's a perspective most feature PMs never get.

我相信好的產品工作是把混亂變成秩序、把知識變成流程、把流程變成組織可以重複使用的能力。 I believe good product work turns chaos into order, knowledge into process, and process into capability an organization can reuse.
