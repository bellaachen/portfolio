About

關於我Who I Am

我通常不是從畫面開始思考產品，而是先理解整個系統。在開始規劃功能之前，我習慣先釐清商業規則、資料流、使用者流程，以及不同系統之間的關聯，再把這些資訊整理成工程團隊可以落地實作的產品規格。 I usually don't start thinking about a product from the screen — I start from the system. Before planning any feature, I first clarify the business rules, data flows, user journeys, and how different systems relate, then turn that understanding into product specs an engineering team can actually build.

過去十年，我參與過金融數據、社群平台、移動出行以及交易平台等不同領域的產品。每一次跨領域，都需要重新理解新的商業模式、新的使用者，以及新的系統規則。這些經驗也讓我更習慣快速建立領域知識，並把複雜的問題整理成清楚的產品方案。 Over the past ten years I've worked on products across fintech data, community platforms, mobility, and trading platforms. Every domain switch meant learning a new business model, new users, and new system rules — experiences that taught me to build domain knowledge quickly and distill complex problems into clear product solutions.

我相信好的產品工作，不只是交付功能。更重要的是，把混亂整理成秩序，把知識沉澱成流程，讓團隊能夠持續累積、重複使用，並一起做出更好的產品。 I believe good product work is about more than shipping features. What matters more is turning chaos into order and distilling knowledge into process, so a team can keep accumulating, keep reusing — and build better products together.
